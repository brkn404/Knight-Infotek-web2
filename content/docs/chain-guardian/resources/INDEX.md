### Mining Pool Case Study

**Scenario**: Large mining pool protecting 10,000+ miners

**Challenge**: Shared liability risk from illegal content

**Solution**: ChainGuardian centralized filtering

**Results**:
- 100% compliance with jurisdiction requirements
- Zero legal incidents
- Competitive advantage in market
- Enhanced reputation

**Results**:
